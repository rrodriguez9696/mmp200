The p5.js is a JavaScript library that starts with the original goal of Processing, to make coding accessible for artists, designers, educators, and beginners, and reinterprets this for today's web. p5 has a full set of drawing functionality. However, you're not limited to your drawing canvas, you can think of your whole browser page as your sketch! p5 also has libraries that make it easy to interact with other HTML5 objects, including text, input, video, webcam, and sound.
